# TryHackMe — Easy

Where it all starts. Easy rooms on TryHackMe are designed to get you through the door — they walk you through the methodology without expecting you to already know it. Every box has a clear attack path and most vulnerabilities are well-documented. The point isn't just to get the flag — it's to understand why the exploit worked and build the habit of structured enumeration.
